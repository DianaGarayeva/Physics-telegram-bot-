<b>Welcome to I_like_physics_bot!</b>

I will help you to study physics step by step

<u> Main commands: </u>
/start - run the bot
/help - show the information about the bot
/menu - open the main menu

<i> How to use: </i>
- press /menu or "Go to the main menu" button
- choose option you like: "self study"/"all section"
- "Self study" is a mode in which you will study physics step by step
- use "All sections" if you need to study a certain section
- study theory, learn formulas, solve problems, take quizzes
Text us with "Send message"
